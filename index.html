About

Now that the 2015-2016 FTC/FRC competition seasons are over, the teams are switching to off-season schedules. Time for projects, learning and outreach at a more relaxed pace.

FRC 1640 had a good season with DEWBOT XII. We were 3rd seed and Finalist at Seneca and 3rd seed and Champion at Westtown. For the first time, we co-hosted an FRC event (Westtown - with 1391 Metal Moose). We participated in MAR and FRC Championships, making it into eliminations in both. The team received three engineering awards during the season.

FTC 7314 seriously stepped up its game in 2016, winning the South Central PA Qualifying Tournament and the PA Championship. The team was Division Finalist at the East Super-Regional Championship Tournament and thereby became the only Pennsylvania team to qualify for the FTC World Championship. The team also received two engineering awards and were finalists at the Eastern Pennsylvania Qualifying Tournament. An outstanding season.

Contact us at FRC1640@gmail.com - FRC 1640 is currently meeting Wednesday evenings 6:00-9:00pm; FTC 7314 is meeting Mondays 6:30-8:30pm; all at the CCIU TCHS Brandywine Campus, 443 Boot Road, Downingtown PA.

FRC 1640

FRC Team 1640 was founded with 12 students in 2005 by Downingtown East High School physics teacher Paul Sabatino. Prior to joining the Downingtown East faculty, Mr. Sabatino mentored FRC Team 104 in the West Chester School District.

The team's name is Sab-BOT-age, in honor of Paul Sabatino's vital contributions.

The FRC 1640 team now encompasses thirty five students from throughout Chester County and more than a dozen mentors. While the organization has expanded its scope to include a FIRST Tech Challenge team and hosts two FIRST LEGO League events annually, FRC 1640 remains solidly the core of Downingtown Area Robotic (DAR).
